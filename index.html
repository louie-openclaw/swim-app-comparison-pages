The sharper story for Peak

If you say this cleanly, the market map gets easier to understand.

What others mostly do

  • MySwimPro / Swim Coach / Swim.com: help swimmers train, track, and stay motivated.
  • Commit: help clubs run admin and coaching operations in one system.
  • Mako: help coaches analyze performance with richer intelligence tooling.
  • Meet Mobile: help everyone consume meet results.
  • TeamCheck: help teams with attendance, communication, and simple ops.

What Peak should claim

  • Not just a swimmer app.
  • Not just admin software.
  • Not just performance analytics.
  • A system for clubs and federations to build a stronger path to success.
  • The layer that connects planning, structure, athletes, coaches, and performance outcomes.
